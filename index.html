Privacy Policy for AktuBaar

Last Updated: March 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to AktuBaar, an educational app designed to provide students with access to study materials, syllabi, question banks, and other academic resources for AKTU (Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

By using AktuBaar, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you use the app, such as:

  • Name and Email Address: When you create a profile or update your profile in the app, you may provide your name and email address. This information is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and is used to personalize your experience (e.g., displaying your name on the Home Screen).
  • Feedback and Issue Reports: If you submit feedback or report an issue through the app, we may collect the content of your message, along with your email address if you choose to provide it, to respond to your inquiry.

Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information that does not directly identify you, including:

  • Device Information: Information about your device, such as device type, operating system, device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID on Android), and IP address.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited, features used, and app usage patterns.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized data about app usage, such as the number of users, session duration, and feature usage, to improve the app's performance and user experience.

Advertising and Third-Party Ad Networks

AktuBaar uses third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob, to display advertisements within the app. These ad networks may collect and use certain information to serve ads, which may include personalized ads based on your interests or non-personalized ads.

Information Collected for Advertising

To provide and improve our advertising services, the following information may be collected by us or our advertising partners:

  • Device Information: Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID on Android), device type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (e.g., city or region) based on your IP address or device settings, if you have granted location permissions.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the app and ads, such as ad impressions, clicks, and app usage patterns.
  • Other Data: Data provided by Google AdMob or other ad networks for ad targeting and analytics purposes.

This data is collected to serve relevant advertisements, measure ad performance, and improve the overall user experience.

Purpose of Data Collection

The data collected for advertising purposes is used to:

  • Display advertisements within the app, which may be personalized based on your interests or non-personalized.
  • Analyze ad performance and user engagement to optimize ad delivery.
  • Support the operation and maintenance of AktuBaar by generating revenue through ad impressions.

User Consent for Personalized Ads

Before displaying personalized ads, AktuBaar requests your consent through the User Messaging Platform (UMP) in compliance with applicable privacy laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You can choose to allow or decline personalized ads during the consent process. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized and based on contextual information rather than your personal data.

Third-Party Sharing

To serve ads, we share the collected data with our advertising partners, such as Google AdMob. These partners may use the data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google AdMob's privacy policy for more information on how they handle your data: Google Privacy Policy.

User Choices and Opt-Out Options

You have control over how your data is used for advertising:

  • Personalized Ads: You can manage your ad personalization settings through the consent form presented in the app or by adjusting your device settings (e.g., resetting your Advertising ID or opting out of ad personalization in your Android settings).
  • Opt-Out of Interest-Based Ads: You can opt out of interest-based ads by visiting the following resources:
    • Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/
    • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): https://optout.networkadvertising.org/
    • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): https://optout.aboutads.info/
  • Do Not Sell My Personal Information (CCPA): If you are a California resident, you can request that we do not sell your personal information by contacting us at support@aktubaar.com.

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not stop ads from being displayed; you will still see non-personalized ads in the app.

Compliance with Privacy Laws

We are committed to complying with applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play policies. We use the User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ensure that users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, and other regions requiring consent are prompted to provide their consent for personalized ads. For users in California, we provide options to opt out of the sale of personal information as required by the CCPA.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To deliver the core functionality of AktuBaar, such as displaying study materials, syllabi, and question banks, and personalizing your experience (e.g., showing your name on the Home Screen).
  • To Improve the App: To analyze usage patterns and feedback to enhance the app's features, performance, and user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: To respond to your feedback, issue reports, or inquiries submitted through the app.
  • For Advertising: To serve ads through third-party ad networks, as described in the "Advertising and Third-Party Ad Networks" section.
  • For Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and improve its functionality using tools like Firebase Analytics.

Third-Party Services

AktuBaar integrates with the following third-party services, which may collect and process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for database storage (to store study materials and question banks), analytics (to track app usage), and crash reporting (to identify and fix app issues). See Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve ads in the app. See the "Advertising and Third-Party Ad Networks" section for details.
  • Coil: We use the Coil library to load images (e.g., profile images in the navigation drawer). Coil does not collect personal data but may log errors for debugging purposes.
  • OkHttp: We use OkHttp to download PDF files (e.g., notes and question banks). OkHttp does not collect personal data but may log network requests for debugging purposes.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Local Storage: Your name and email address (if provided) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you submit feedback or report an issue.
  • Firebase Database: Study materials, question banks, and other app content are stored in Firebase Realtime Database. Firebase implements security measures to protect this data, as outlined in Firebase's Privacy and Security documentation.
  • Downloads: When you download PDF files (e.g., notes or question banks), they are saved to your device's external storage in the Downloads directory. You are responsible for managing these files on your device.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Local Data: Your name and email address are stored on your device until you log out or uninstall the app.
  • Feedback and Issue Reports: If you submit feedback or report an issue, we retain the content of your message and any associated contact information (e.g., email address) for as long as necessary to address your inquiry, after which it may be deleted or anonymized.
  • Analytics and Ad Data: Usage data collected for analytics and advertising purposes is retained in accordance with the policies of our third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase, Google AdMob).

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and update your name and email address directly in the app's Profile screen.
  • Deletion: You can delete your local data by logging out of the app (via the navigation drawer) or uninstalling the app. To request the deletion of feedback or issue report data, please contact us at support@aktubaar.com.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: See the "Advertising and Third-Party Ad Networks" section for instructions on opting out of personalized ads.
  • CCPA Rights (California Residents): If you are a California resident, you have the right to request that we disclose the categories of personal information we collect, use, or sell, and to request that we do not sell your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@aktubaar.com.
  • GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Residents): If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@aktubaar.com.

Children's Privacy

AktuBaar is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at support@aktubaar.com.

The updated policy will be posted at https://5aurabhdubey.github.io/aktubaar-privacy-policy/, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy will be revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the app after the updated policy is posted constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
