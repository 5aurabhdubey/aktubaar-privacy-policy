Privacy Policy for AktuBaar

Last Updated: April 09, 2025

Welcome to AktuBaar, an educational application designed to assist students with AKTU syllabus access, question banks, community features, and more. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our app. AktuBaar now includes advertisements via Google AdMob, which involves additional data collection and sharing with third parties. This policy is managed in compliance with the User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK for consent. By using AktuBaar, you agree to the terms described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and enhance our services:

a. Personal Information

  • Username and Email: When you register or use the app, you may provide a username and email address. This data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and is used to personalize your experience (e.g., displaying your username) and associate it with community posts or feedback.
  • Feedback and Issue Reports: If you submit feedback or report issues via the app, we collect your username, email, and the content of your submission to address your concerns and improve the app.

b. User-Generated Content

  • Community Posts: When you post in the Community section, we collect your username, post content, and a timestamp. This data is stored in Firebase Firestore to enable user interaction.

c. Device and Usage Information

  • Firebase Data: We use Firebase (a Google service) for data storage, crash reporting, and app management. Firebase may automatically collect device information, such as device ID, app instance ID, and crash logs, to ensure app stability and performance.
  • External Links: The app links to external websites (e.g., aktu.ac.in for syllabus and results). We do not collect data from these interactions, but the linked sites may have their own privacy policies.
  • Advertising Data: AktuBaar now uses Google AdMob for banner advertisements. AdMob and its partners may collect:
    • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID).
    • IP address and approximate location (derived from IP).
    • App usage data and inferred interests for ad personalization.
    This data is collected with your consent via the User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK and is used to deliver targeted ads. You can review Google’s AdMob policies at support.google.com/admob.

d. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal data (e.g., aggregated usage statistics) if analytics are enabled in the future. Any such collection will be disclosed in an updated policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Personalization: To display your username and tailor your app experience.
  • App Functionality: To store and display community posts and educational resources via Firebase.
  • Improvement: To process feedback and issues to enhance the app.
  • Security and Maintenance: Firebase uses device data for crash reporting and app stability.
  • Advertising: To deliver personalized ads through Google AdMob, subject to your consent via the UMP SDK.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

a. Storage

  • Local Storage: Username, email, and preferences (e.g., dark mode) are stored on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Cloud Storage: Community posts, feedback, and educational resources are stored in Firebase Firestore and Realtime Database.
  • Ad Data: Advertising data is managed by Google AdMob and its partners, stored according to their privacy policies, and governed by the UMP SDK.

b. Security

  • Firebase provides encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest to protect your data.
  • Google AdMob uses industry-standard security for ad-related data.
  • We implement secure coding practices to minimize risks.
  • However, no data transmission or storage is entirely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access or breaches.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information but may share it with third parties as follows:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for data storage and crash reporting. See Google’s Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google AdMob: AdMob and its partners process advertising data for ad delivery. This is governed by the UMP SDK with your consent. Review AdMob’s Privacy Policy at support.google.com/admob.
  • External Websites: Links to sites like aktu.ac.in are not monitored by us. Their privacy practices apply.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect AktuBaar, its users, or the public.
